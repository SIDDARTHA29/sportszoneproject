About the College Hosting the Sports Event

Kamala Institute of Technology and Science is a premier educational institution known for its commitment to academic excellence and physical fitness. With state-of-the-art sports facilities, we aim to foster talent, teamwork, and discipline among students. Our annual sports event brings together athletes from all disciplines to showcase their skills, fostering a spirit of healthy competition and camaraderie.

Join us as we celebrate sportsmanship and achievement, creating memories that will last a lifetime. Whether you're a participant or a spectator, the event promises an exciting and inspiring experience.
